What Did I Learn from The Mindfulness Conversation

We learnt that we could be in charge of how we RESPOND to our thoughts, emotions, as they arise. So the trick is to not control our emotions or thoughts, but to control our emotions

Techniques to Practice

Just breathing and focusing on my breath. And watching the thoughts etc arise and being gentle.

Further Thoughs

"I am in control of my responses." - I just have to keep repeating that every day.
