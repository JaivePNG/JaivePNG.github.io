Search Inside Yourself

What Did I Learn from The Mindfulness Conversation

We learnt that we could be in charge of how we RESPOND to our thoughts, emotions, as they arise. So the real power is to not control our emotions or thoughts, but to control our emotions. On such mindfulness conversations, we can realise when we are present and contributing in a conversation or whether we are just going through the motions. This presence and control of our participation helps to really listen and to understand what someone else is saying, and also helps us improve the depth of our responses. I feel that this is an area that I have failed at in my life, I need to give accurate answers or those deep enough to reflect my situation and at the same time I am never fully present in a conversation. Mindfulness conversation is a new and wonderful practice that I have tried to adopt everyday, though it is a struggle.

Techniques to Practice

1. Just breathing and focusing on my breath. And watching the thoughts etc arise and being gentle. 2. Listening first, thinking as deep as I can go, and responding. 3. Listening to my 'first' response, before I have given it, and asking myself, is this truly a honest answer that touches on my values.

Further Thoughs

I want to always become a better person everyday, a more richer and deeper person. Mindfulness conversation is one of the tools I am using.
