What Is Process over Product

Process over product means you start designing a better user experience without a line of code written. You think about the websites goals, such as who is the audience, how will the audience interact with the webiste, what are the goals of the website, where will it be accessed (mobile, pc etc). What kind of content will the websitye contain. Process over product helps you desing and develop a purposeful website.
